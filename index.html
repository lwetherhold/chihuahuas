How we rank matches

Each dog gets a match score (0–100) from weight (closer to ~5–6 lbs is better), how cuddly they seem, and gentle penalties if the listing sounds very high-energy. You can override the score in the data file when you disagree.

  • Excellent 85–100
  • Strong 70–84
  • Good 55–69
  • Okay 40–54
  • Tougher fit under 40
